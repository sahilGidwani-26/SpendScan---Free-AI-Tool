# ECONOMICS

## What's a converted lead worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume:
- Average deal size: $3,000/year in credits (conservative — a 10-person startup replacing Cursor Business + Claude Team is ~$600/mo → $7,200/yr)
- Credex margin on credits: ~15% (buying distressed inventory at 20-25% discount, selling at 5-10% discount to customer)
- **LTV per customer = $3,000 × 15% = $450 gross profit/customer/year**
- With a 2-year average retention: **$900 LTV**

## CAC at each channel

| Channel | Est. conversion rate | CAC |
|---------|---------------------|-----|
| HN Show HN → audit → lead | 0.8% of visitors | $0 (time cost ~$40 equivalent) |
| Reddit organic | 1.2% of visitors | $0 (time cost ~$20/post) |
| Twitter thread | 0.5% of impressions become audits; 15% audit→lead | ~$0 ($10 time) |
| Existing Credex customer email | 30% open, 10% audit, 40% lead conversion | $0 (already have list) |
| Paid Twitter/X ads (if later) | 2% CTR, 20% audit completion, 15% lead | ~$180/lead at $0.50 CPC |

**Organic CAC: ~$30-60 (time cost only). Paid CAC: ~$180.**

At $900 LTV, even paid acquisition at $180 is a 5:1 LTV:CAC — healthy.

## Conversion funnel

```
Landing page visit: 100%
  → Audit started: 45% (form friction filter)
  → Audit completed: 75% of starters = 34% of visitors
  → Email capture: 20% of completers = 7% of visitors
  → Credex consultation booked (high-savings only): 15% of leads = 1% of visitors
  → Credit purchase: 40% of consultations = 0.4% of visitors
```

**With 1,000 visitors/month:**
- 340 audits
- 68 leads
- ~10 consultation bookings (assuming 30% show high-savings)
- 4 credit purchases
- 4 × $450 gross margin = **$1,800/month from 1,000 visitors**

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

**What needs to be true:**

1. **Scale to 10,000 audits/month by month 6.** 
   - Month 1: 500 (organic HN + Reddit)
   - Month 3: 2,000 (word of mouth + Credex existing customers)
   - Month 6: 10,000 (if one viral tweet or a YC partner recommends it)

2. **Maintain 20% audit → lead conversion.** 
   - 10,000 audits → 2,000 leads/month

3. **Convert 3% of leads to paying customers.**
   - 2,000 leads × 3% = 60 new customers/month

4. **At $3,000 ACV average deal size:**
   - 60 customers × $3,000 = $180,000 new ARR/month
   - By month 18: **$180k × 12 months = $2.16M ARR** (hits $1M around month 9-10)

**The key assumption that breaks this:** Conversion rate from audit → consultation → purchase. At 3% it works. At 1% we're at $720k ARR at month 18 — close but under. This means the consultation booking flow and Credex sales follow-up are the most important thing to optimize.

**What $1M ARR actually requires:**
~333 active credit-buying customers at $3k ACV. That's 333 startups — entirely achievable in the TAM of 100,000+ startups globally running AI tooling.
