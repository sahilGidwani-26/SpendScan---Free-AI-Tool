## Assumptions and notes

1. **Enterprise pricing** — Where vendors don't publish enterprise pricing, estimates are based on industry-standard enterprise uplift (1.5x–2x business plan) and publicly reported figures. Estimates are clearly marked.
2. **API pricing** — API usage is variable and depends on call volume, model choice, and prompt length. Optimization recommendations are percentage-based rather than absolute.
3. **Team plan minimums** — Claude Team (5-seat min) and ChatGPT Team (2-seat min) minimums are factored into recommendations.
